Who is Seulgi?

Seulgi is a South Korean singer and performer who gained fame as a member of the popular South Korean girl group Red Velvet. She was born as Kang Seul-gi on February 10, 1994, in Ansan, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. Seulgi is known for her versatile talents, which include singing, dancing, and even songwriting.

As a member of Red Velvet, Seulgi has contributed to the group's success with hit songs like "Russian Roulette," "Red Flavor," "Bad Boy," and many others. Red Velvet is renowned for its unique blend of pop, R&B, and electronic music styles, and Seulgi's stage presence and charisma have played a significant role in the group's popularity.

In addition to her work with Red Velvet, Seulgi has also pursued solo projects and collaborations, showcasing her talents and versatility as an artist. She has garnered a significant fan following and is recognized for her strong vocals, exceptional dancing skills, and charismatic personality in the world of K-pop.
